The Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is a breathtaking natural wonder located in Arizona, United States. It is a steep-sided canyon carved by the Colorado River, and is over 277 miles long and up to 18 miles wide.

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built along the northern borders of China to protect against invaders. The wall is made of stone, brick, tamped earth, wood, and other materials, and is the longest wall in the world, stretching over 13,000 miles.

The Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient Egyptian monuments located on the outskirts of Cairo. They were built over 4,500 years ago as tombs for pharaohs and their consorts, and are one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
